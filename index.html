Sustainable Development Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

SDG 11 aims to make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. It focuses on improving urban planning, management, and reducing the environmental impact of cities. Achieving this goal involves providing access to safe and affordable housing, transportation, and green public spaces, while also enhancing disaster resilience and preserving cultural and natural heritage.

This goal is crucial for fostering economic growth, ensuring social inclusion, and promoting environmental sustainability in urban areas worldwide.
